Here’s ClientFlow, a client delivery workspace I built for service businesses. Instead of chasing project updates across email, folders, and spreadsheets, the owner and client share one clear view. Let me show you.

On the owner dashboard, I can see active work, open requests, and outstanding invoices. I’ll open the project plan and mark visual design approval complete. Progress updates immediately, and the action is recorded.

Now I’ll switch to the client side. The client can send a clear request without starting another email thread. I’ll ask for a launch countdown, add the context, and send it to the project owner.

Back in the owner view, I can confirm payment on the sent invoice. Finally, the activity timeline shows every important change: who did it, what changed, and when.
